Tomato soup recipe

This simple and absolutely delicious tomato soup recipe will undoubtedly save the day. Or night! Let's see how easy it can get.

Ingredients

  • Toro tomato soup pack
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Eggs
  • Basil leaves

Steps

  1. Boil up the water and cream mixture
  2. Add the tomato soup pack's content
  3. Let simmer for about 7 minutes
  4. Ready to serve with hard boiled eggs in slices and top with basil leaves
  5. Enjoy!
