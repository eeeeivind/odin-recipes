Pizza recipe

This simple and absolutely delicious pizza recipe will undoubtedly save the day. Or night! Let's see how easy it can get.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Sauce ingredients
  • No-boil noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Heat the oil and cook the ground beef
  3. Add tomatoes, tomato paste and seasoning
  4. Let simmer for five minutes
  5. Add layers of noodles, ground beef mixture, and cheeses in a baking dish
  6. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 35 to 45 minutes
  7. Remove foil and continue to bake for an additional 10 minutes
  8. Let the dish rest for 10 minutes before serving and top with chipped parsley, if desired
